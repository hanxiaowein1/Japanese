# Some people have one career throughout their lives. Other people do different kinds of work at different points in their lives. Which do you think is better? Explain why.
In my opinion, sticking to one career is a better choice for two main reasons.

First, focusing on a single field allows you to develop deep expertise. For example, senior software engineers who specialize in AI design are much harder to replace than junior programmers who only do basic coding. Without this expertise, professionals risk losing their jobs to younger or more skilled workers.

Second, with AI becoming more advanced, many simple tasks can now be automated. Jobs that require only basic skills, like entry-level coding, are increasingly done by machines. However, roles that need advanced problem-solving or creativity are still in demand.

That’s why I believe committing to one career is the smarter choice in the long run.

私の考えでは、一つの職業を長く続けることは二つの主な理由から、より良い選択です。

第一に、一つの分野に専念することで、深い専門知識を身につけることができます。例えば、AI 設計を専門とする上級ソフトウェアエンジニアは、基礎的なコーディングしかできない初級プログラマーよりも、代替されにくいです。こうした専門性がなければ、社会人は若い人やよりスキルの高い人に職を奪われるリスクが生じます。

第二に、AI の技術が高度化するにつれ、単純な作業の多くが自動化されるようになりました。
